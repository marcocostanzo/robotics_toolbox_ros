166 %TR2RPY/R2RPY Convert a homogeneous transform / Rotation Matrix to roll-pitch-yaw angles

168 % RPY = TR2RPY(T) are the roll-pitch-yaw angles (1x3)
169 % corresponding to the rotation part of a homogeneous transform T. The 3
